---

## Overview

Retail businesses generate large volumes of operational data every day, making it challenging for decision-makers to monitor performance efficiently. This project transforms raw retail data into a centralized Business Intelligence dashboard, enabling executives and operations teams to track key metrics, identify operational risks, and make faster, data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Retail managers need a single source of truth to answer critical business questions such as:

- How is the business performing overall?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which products require immediate replenishment?
- Which warehouses are operating near capacity?
- Where is procurement spending concentrated?

Without an integrated dashboard, these insights are often scattered across multiple reports, slowing operational decision-making.

---

## Solution

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard integrated with SQL Server to provide real-time visibility into retail operations. The solution combines executive KPIs, sales performance, inventory monitoring, procurement analysis, warehouse utilization, and product performance into a single reporting platform.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Achieved **$174.56M** in total revenue with an overall **50.25% profit margin**.
- **Home Goods** was the highest-performing category, generating approximately **$56M** in revenue.
- Identified **61 Low Stock SKUs** and **26 Out of Stock SKUs**, enabling proactive inventory replenishment.
- Procurement spending was primarily concentrated among suppliers from **Malaysia** and **Morocco**.
- Revenue increased consistently throughout the year, growing from **$12.43M** in January to **$21.32M** in December.
